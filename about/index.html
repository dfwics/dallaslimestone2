About

Twenty-five years of stone that stays put

Dallas Limestone opened in 1999 in Mesquite, Texas, cutting cantera columns for a handful of custom home builders in the eastern suburbs of Dallas. Cantera — a volcanic stone that carves and turns cleaner than most limestone — is still what we're best known for, but the shop grew into full-service stone fabrication because customers kept asking for the next thing: a matching header, then a fountain, then a full facade.

Twenty-five years later we still cut, carve, and install everything ourselves. No brokered stone, no subcontracted installation crews. When a piece leaves our yard, the same people who cut it are the ones setting it.
