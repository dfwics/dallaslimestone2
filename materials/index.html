Materials

Stone, sorted by what it's for

Everything here is cut and finished at our Mesquite shop. Filter by type, or scroll down for the full detail on each one.

materials/cantera-columns-01.webp

Cantera Columns

Cantera is a volcanic stone that turns and carves cleaner than most limestone, which is why it's been our founding specialty since 1999. We cut column shafts and capitals in classic proportions or to a custom order, then match bases and pedestals so the whole set reads as one piece.

materials/cantera-carved-panel-01.webp

Carved Cantera Panels

Relief-carved medallions, cartouches, and house-number plaques cut to a pattern you bring in or one of ours. Panels ship pre-drilled for anchoring and sealed for exterior use.

materials/indiana-limestone-01.webp

Indiana Limestone

A fine, even grain with almost no color variation between blocks, which is why architects specify it for trim and banding where consistency matters more than character.

materials/texas-limestone-01.webp

Texas Cream Limestone

Quarried in the Hill Country and the warm tone most North Texas builders picture when they say "limestone." We keep a rotating stock on hand rather than special-ordering every job.

materials/carrara-marble-01.webp

Carrara Marble

Soft grey veining on a white ground, book-matched where the application calls for it. Common on tub decks, vanity tops, and fireplace surrounds that need a quieter finish than cantera.

materials/emperador-marble-01.webp

Emperador Marble

A deep brown marble with heavier veining than Carrara, most requested for fireplace surrounds and bar tops where the stone itself is the focal point of the room.

materials/balustrade-01.webp

Balustrades & Newels

Turned or hand-carved baluster sets cut to match an existing stair run or built fresh for a terrace edge, sized so the rhythm of the spacing looks right from the street.

materials/window-surround-01.webp

Window & Door Surrounds

Cast or hand-carved surrounds, keystones, and quoins that give a flat elevation real shadow lines — the detail that separates a facade from a veneer.

materials/thin-veneer-01.webp

Thin Stone Veneer

Lightweight veneer panels for retrofits and remodels where the structure can't carry full-bed stone. Same face, a fraction of the load.

materials/full-bed-facade-01.webp

Full-Bed Facades

Structural cladding cut to spec for commercial storefronts, entries, and monument signage, engineered and anchored as a full system rather than a surface finish.
